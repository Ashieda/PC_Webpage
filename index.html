General Rules

Code of conduct to follow in guild chat & any guild activities

  1. Toxicity is not permitted. What defines toxicity is up to the leadership, but you can generally expect this to include name-calling, excessive swearing, excessive aggression and any kind of prejudiced speech or behavior.
  2. Politics and religion are off-limits in public forums such as guild chat, raid chat or raid Discord channels. You’re more than welcome to discuss these things at length in private with people who want to discuss them with you, but leave them out of the general lanes of communication.
  3. Keep the guild bank tidy. This means putting things in the tabs that are appropriate for them, and not putting junk in the guild bank. If you’re not sure if something belongs or where it belongs, ask an officer for help.

Raid Rules

Loot & character rules for raid

  1. Loot priority is largely left to the in-game system, where Need > Greed. Officers will make rulings for certain edge cases when the system's behavior is unsatisfactory or if a particular item is especially strong for a certain class, but do not expect this treatment routinely, if ever. The goal is to finish the raid. There will always be more opportunities for loot.
  2. Alts are not permitted for content that is not on farm.
  3. One character swap is allowed per person per season, given that the new character is of the same role as the player's first and within 5 item levels of the original character. Exceptions to the role rule will be permitted if two players want to swap roles and both meet the item level rule.
  4. All conduct rules from the above section apply in raid as well.
